How to Make Mochi

Mochi is a tasty dessert, that has a super fun chewy texture!
Some mochi has fillings such as red bean, black sesame, etc, but
today we are going to learn how to make the classic base!

First things first: Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • handful of potato starch

And that's all you need! The method, we'll be using today
is a super simple and fast one in the microwave, but there
are versions that use steamers

Procedure

  1. Add flour and sugar to a bowl and give it a mix
  2. Then add the water and stir until combined
  3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 1 min
  4. Remove from microwave and stir with a spatula
  5. Repeat once more, and then one more time but only for 30 seconds
  6. Dust area with potato starch and dump mochi on top
  7. After dusting the top of the mochi roll it out
  8. Cut into cubes and enjoy!
  9. Optionally, you could cut boxes and add a red bean filling
    and seal the edges to make a filled mochi ball

I hope you give it a shot, it's super quick to make and very enjoyable :)
